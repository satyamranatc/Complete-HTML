Black Hole
A black hole is a region of spacetime where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape from it. It is a point of infinite density and is the largest object in the universe with no known boundary.
Space Nova
A space nova is a supernova explosion that occurs when a star collapses under its own gravity, causing a massive explosion that releases a large amount of energy in the form of light, heat, and radiation.
